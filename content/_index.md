# Practical RMF, eMASS, and Cyber Compliance Notes

WellScript documents practical lessons in RMF, eMASS, POA&Ms, ACAS findings, evidence preparation, continuous monitoring, Splunk analysis, and federal cybersecurity compliance.

The site is built for sysadmins, new ISSOs, RMF analysts, and cybersecurity professionals who want plain-language guidance on how technical findings become documentation, evidence, POA&Ms, and authorization support.

Built by a U.S. Air Force veteran and federal IT professional who spent years making systems work before learning how to make them defensible.
